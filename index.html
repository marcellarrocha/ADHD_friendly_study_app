Topic 01: Operations & Process Management

Welcome to the first topic in Operations and Process Management. This dashboard is designed to help you synthesize the core concepts of process design, structural layout, and operational strategy. Operations management is the function responsible for the design, management, and improvement of processes that create and deliver products or services. Every organization uses an input-transformation-output model. Here, you will actively explore how varying operational profiles dictate different business strategies.

📥

Inputs

Transformed resources (Materials, Information, Customers) & Transforming resources (Facilities, Staff, Capital).

⚙

Transformation

The network of activities and buffers that treat, convert, or process the inputs.

📤

Outputs

The final products and services delivered to satisfy customer and market demands.
